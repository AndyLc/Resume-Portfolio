EigenBeat

An investigation on using Principal Component Analysis to find "eigenbeats" using hip-hop drum beats. The idea was inspired by the concept of an eigenface, the representation of face data at the orthogonal intercept of a space spanned by eigenvectors. Instead of using images, I use music to try to construct a kind of "eigenbeat."
